Meet Wanda

Committed to Compassionate Senior Care

Wanda K. Miller is the heart and founder of Miller Senior Care Clinic. Based in Springfield, Missouri, Wanda has spent over 12 years dedicating her life to providing professional, personalized and compassionate care for seniors right in the comfort of their own homes.

Her journey began with a simple belief — that every senior deserves to age with dignity, safety and joy. What started as a passion for helping elderly neighbors has grown into a trusted clinic serving hundreds of families across Springfield, Ozark, Republic and surrounding communities.

Wanda combines her clinical training with a warm, family-first approach. She doesn't just provide care — she builds meaningful relationships with every senior and family she serves, ensuring that trust, respect and genuine connection are always at the center of everything she does.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every decision, every visit and every care plan we create is guided by these deeply held values.

Compassion

We treat every senior with genuine warmth, patience and deep empathy — exactly as we would care for our own family.

Dignity

Every senior deserves respect and independence. We deliver care in a way that always preserves personal dignity.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standard of professional care, always going above and beyond what is expected.

Trust

Families trust us with their most precious loved ones. We honor that trust with honesty, reliability and transparency.

Family-Centered

We keep families informed and involved every step of the way — because great senior care is a team effort.

Home Comfort

There is no place like home. We bring professional clinical care directly to where seniors feel safest and happiest.

Qualifications

Trained, Certified & Experienced

CPR & First Aid Certified

Fully certified in CPR, AED use and First Aid to respond quickly and effectively in any emergency situation.

Medication Management Trained

Specialized training in safe medication administration, scheduling and documentation for senior patients.
