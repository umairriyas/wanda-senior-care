Our Senior Care Services

Comprehensive, compassionate in-home care services tailored to every senior's unique needs in Springfield, Missouri.

Home Services
Expert Care

Everything Your Senior Needs at Home

Wanda K. Miller delivers a full range of professional senior home care services — from daily health monitoring to warm companionship — all in the comfort of home.

Most Requested

Health Monitoring

Regular, professional health assessments to track vitals, identify concerns early and ensure seniors remain safe and healthy at home.

  • Blood pressure & heart rate checks
  • Blood sugar & oxygen monitoring
  • Weekly health progress reports
  • Physician coordination & updates
Critical Care

Medication Management

Precise medication scheduling, reminders and administration support to ensure seniors never miss a dose or take incorrect medications.

  • Daily medication reminders
  • Dosage tracking & documentation
  • Prescription refill coordination
  • Side-effect monitoring & reporting

Personal Care Assistance

Gentle, respectful assistance with daily personal hygiene routines to help seniors maintain cleanliness, comfort and self-confidence.

  • Bathing & personal hygiene
  • Dressing & grooming assistance
  • Oral care & skincare support
  • Toileting & incontinence care

Companion Care

Meaningful social interaction, conversation and activities to keep seniors mentally stimulated, emotionally connected and free from loneliness.

  • Friendly conversation & engagement
  • Games, reading & light activities
  • Errand running & accompaniment
  • Emotional support & companionship

Mobility & Therapy Support

Guided movement exercises and light physical therapy assistance to help seniors maintain strength, balance and independence.

  • Gentle daily exercise routines
  • Balance & fall prevention training
  • Post-surgery mobility assistance
  • Safe transfer & repositioning

Nutrition & Meal Support

Healthy, delicious meal preparation and dietary guidance designed to meet each senior's specific nutritional and health requirements.

  • Customized meal planning
  • Grocery shopping assistance
  • Diabetic & special diet meals
  • Hydration monitoring
Specialized

Dementia & Memory Care

Specialized, patient care for seniors living with Alzheimer's, dementia and other memory conditions — delivered with exceptional kindness.

  • Alzheimer's & dementia care
  • Cognitive stimulation activities
  • Safe environment supervision
  • Behavioral support & redirection

Post-Hospital Recovery Care

Dedicated care and support during the critical recovery period after hospital stays or surgical procedures to prevent re-hospitalization.

  • Wound care & dressing changes
  • Recovery exercise assistance
  • Follow-up appointment coordination
  • Family status updates

Light Housekeeping

Maintaining a clean, safe and organized living environment so seniors can enjoy a comfortable and healthy home every day.

  • Tidying & light cleaning
  • Laundry & linen changes
  • Dishes & kitchen cleaning
  • Safe home hazard checks
